  <section class="home-section" id="research">
    <div class="section-heading">
      <p class="section-kicker">Selected Research</p>
      <h2>科研与在研工作</h2>
      <p>首页只展示当前最能代表我研究取向的工作，重点放在问题、方法和证据。</p>
    </div>

    <div class="entry-stack">
      <article class="entry-card entry-card--featured">
        <div class="entry-header">
          <p class="entry-period">2025.10 - 2026.03</p>
          <span class="entry-badge">ECCV 2026 Submission</span>
        </div>
        <h3>Reliability-Aware 3D Geometric Injection for Universal Person Re-identification</h3>
        <div class="point-grid">
          <div>
            <span>问题</span>
            <p>Universal ReID 在遮挡、换装、跨模态等复杂设定下，3D 几何先验常常不稳定，甚至会引入负迁移。</p>
          </div>
          <div>
            <span>方法</span>
            <p>基于 SMPL 构建全局形状与关节拓扑表示，并引入 Reliability-Aware Gate 完成 2D-3D 自适应融合。</p>
          </div>
          <div>
            <span>证据</span>
            <p>完成 9 个 ReID benchmark 的统一训练、对比实验与消融分析，重点验证几何先验何时有效、何时失效。</p>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="evidence-list">
          <span>SMPL geometry</span>
          <span>adaptive fusion</span>
          <span>9 benchmarks</span>
        </div>
      </article>

      <article class="entry-card">
        <div class="entry-header">
          <p class="entry-period">2026.03 - 至今</p>
          <span class="entry-badge">Planned EMNLP Submission</span>
        </div>
        <h3>GraphLite: High-Quality Supervision Enables Deployable Small Models for Graph Extraction</h3>
        <div class="point-grid">
          <div>
            <span>问题</span>
            <p>scientific graph extraction 依赖大模型直接抽取时成本高、稳定性弱，而小模型往往缺乏高质量结构监督。</p>
          </div>
          <div>
            <span>方法</span>
            <p>基于 ARCHE 衍生监督信号构建图谱抽取框架，强调图结构一致性、证据对齐与部署效率。</p>
          </div>
          <div>
            <span>当前进展</span>
            <p>已完成结构化监督构建、评测流程验证与 0.8B 级训练流程打通，正在继续推进模型质量与泛化能力。</p>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="evidence-list">
          <span>high-quality supervision</span>
          <span>0.8B pipeline</span>
          <span>deployable extraction</span>
        </div>
      </article>

      <article class="entry-card">
        <div class="entry-header">
          <p class="entry-period">2026.03 - 至今</p>
          <span class="entry-badge">TPAMI Survey Preparation</span>
        </div>
        <h3>外观变化条件下行人重识别综述与评测指标研究</h3>
        <div class="point-grid">
          <div>
            <span>关注点</span>
            <p>围绕服装变化、光照变化、姿态变化等 appearance variation 场景，重新审视任务定义、方法分类与评测局限。</p>

          </div>
          <div>
            <span>当前阶段</span>
